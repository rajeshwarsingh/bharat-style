## Usage Guidelines

### WhatsApp Status
- Update 2-3 times per week
- Rotate through different stories
- Keep captions conversational and personal
- Include story link directly in caption
- Post during active hours (evenings, weekends)

### Instagram Stories
- Post 3-4 times per week
- Use story hero images or lifestyle photos
- Add "Link in bio" or swipe-up link if available
- Include visual elements: arrows, highlights
- Tag stories with relevant topics (sustainable fashion, handmade, etc.)

### General Best Practices
- **Timing:** Evening hours (7-9 PM) and weekends work best
- **Frequency:** Don't overwhelm‚Äîquality over quantity
- **Engagement:** Respond to DMs and comments about stories
- **Mix it up:** Rotate through different story topics
- **Track:** Monitor which stories get most engagement

### Content Themes to Rotate
1. Personal stories and anecdotes
2. Artisan stories and craftsmanship
3. Values and mindful choices
4. Sustainable fashion movement
5. Indian heritage and traditions
6. Quality over quantity
7. Women empowerment (subtle)
8. Real vs. fast fashion
9. Natural materials and fabrics
10. Making a difference
